Our Mission

SkillUp Kampala is a community-driven platform designed to tackle youth unemployment in Kampala and Wakiso by making vocational training accessible to everyone.

The Problem

Many youth ages 18-25 lack access to organized information about technical courses, leading to a "skills gap" despite the availability of training centers.

The Solution

We provide a centralized portal where youth can search, filter by location, and apply for courses like coding, tailoring, and mechanics in under three clicks.
